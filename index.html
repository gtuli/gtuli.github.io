Featured initiatives

Each project pairs rigorous analytics with compassionate healthcare delivery—spanning biosurveillance, precision diagnostics, and clinical decision support.

Pandemic Pulse — Digital Biosurveillance

Unified social media, search trends, and news data to detect emerging bio-threats weeks ahead of traditional surveillance, winning DHS’s Hidden Signals Challenge.

  • Role: Principal investigator & systems architect
  • Focus: Weak-signal detection, NLP pipelines, real-time dashboards

ML-Allergy — Food Allergy Risk Stratification

Built a composite biomarker panel powered by machine learning to safely predict oral food challenge outcomes and personalize allergy care at Boston Children’s Hospital.

  • Role: Clinical data scientist
  • Focus: EHR integration, model interpretability, clinician workflows

Pediatric Vision AI — Early Screening

Partnered with ophthalmology teams to translate predictive models and computer vision into a point-of-care vision screening tool for pediatric populations.

  • Role: Product & analytics lead
  • Focus: Computer vision, embedded hardware, clinical validation

ICU Hypoxemia Prediction — Project Apollo

Delivered a bedside decision support platform that forecasts critical oxygen drops minutes before they occur, allowing clinicians to intervene early for vulnerable patients.

  • Role: Principal product owner
  • Focus: Time-series forecasting, clinician-in-the-loop design

Maternal-Infant Health Data Integration

Connected siloed public health, clinical, and social care datasets to close gaps in services for mothers and newborns across state health systems.

  • Role: Program director
  • Focus: Interoperability, community health analytics, policy impact

Global COVID-19 Impact Monitoring

Scaled the 114-country Facebook UMD-CTIS survey to deliver daily COVID-19 situational awareness and validate community symptom signals against official data.

  • Role: Global survey program co-lead
  • Focus: Participatory epidemiology, real-time analytics

Bangkok Vaccine Hesitancy Insights

Provided weekly intelligence on COVID-19 vaccine sentiment for Bangkok’s third wave, surfacing primary concerns and trusted messengers to guide policy.

  • Role: Lead data scientist
  • Focus: Urban health analytics, behavior change

Federated NPI Behavior Study

Partnered with Google Health to deploy a privacy-preserving cohort that tracked masking and distancing behaviors alongside vaccination milestones nationwide.

  • Role: Epidemiology & analytics lead
  • Focus: Federated analysis, differential privacy

COVID-19 Trial Leadership Equity

Audited gender representation across thousands of US COVID-19 trials, benchmarking progress against other diseases and linking leadership to participant diversity.

  • Role: Principal investigator
  • Focus: Clinical data mining, equity analytics

Variant-Responsive Vaccine Effectiveness

Built a rapid estimation pipeline using global Facebook survey data to quantify vaccine performance shifts as Delta and Omicron waves unfolded in LMICs.

  • Role: Study architect
  • Focus: Survey-based VE modeling, global health
